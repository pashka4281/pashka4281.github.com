I heard a shop full of assistants doing their best Kanako schtick, so a senryu haiku came to mind: 


> 


君は今


kimi wa ima


加奈子さんより


kanako san yori


柳原


yanagihara





What I mean is, "you're more Yanagihara than Yanagihara Kanako".
